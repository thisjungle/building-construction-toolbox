Introduction

Welcome to the Building and Construction Toolbox! This comprehensive suite of tools helps you manage various aspects of employee management and compliance with the Building and Construction Industry Award.

Available Tools

  • Quick Award Guide: Learn the basics of the Building and Construction Award
  • Pay Levels: Configure and manage employee classification levels
  • Allowances: Calculate and manage employee allowances
  • Create Position: Create detailed job positions with classifications
  • Pay Calculator: Calculate employee pay with allowances

Important Note

These tools provide guidance only. Always verify calculations and classifications against the official award document and consult with relevant authorities for specific cases.
